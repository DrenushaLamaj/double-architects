About Us

Double Architects is a Kosovo-based design studio specializing in timeless interiors, contemporary architecture, and refined living environments. Led by MSc. Architect Drenushë Lamaj, our studio is rooted in a passion for form, material, and space—delivering calm, functional, and inspiring atmospheres tailored to every client.

We have successfully completed projects in Kosovo, Albania, Switzerland, and Austria, combining international design insight with local expertise.
